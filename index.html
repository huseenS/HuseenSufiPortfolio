Huseen Sufi | Aspiring Web Developer and Software Engineer

I am a quick learner, relentless and thorough individual. I hold myself to high standards and always set ambitious but achievable goals. I've loved problem-solving since my first foray into building computers as a kid then later learning to code simple bash scripts for fun. Now, as a rising Senior at Colorado College, my ambitions include working for a company that is at the leading edge of technology and building upon my skill set by learning new frameworks, languages, and best coding practices. In my free time you'll catch me studying Japanese to keep my conversational ability up and playing videogames. Outside of College and Programming I'm very much into all things tech. I play Ultimate Frisbee and love the outdoors--go hiking and running. Would ride my longboard if my ankle wasn't broken.
